What is BookARtify?

BookARtify is a social media platform dedicated solely to the book community and its talented artists. Instead of having to sift through countless irrelevent results when searching for fanart on larger platforms, BookARtify ensures users will find exactly what they're looking for.


As the name suggests, the app includes an AR feature, allowing artists and bookworms to experience 2D art in a 3D environment.

Features

Upload and share fanart and fan-covers

  • Tag each post with its associated book
  • Crop your image using in-app tool
  • Label your post as "cover" or "artwork"

Follow books and artists

  • Use the ISBN scanner or search keywords (title, author, etc.)
  • Follow books to see tagged art in your feed
  • Discover new artist profiles and explore their work

Experience Augmented Reality (AR)

  • Place art on a 3D book model or in a picture frame depending on its "cover" or "artwork" tag
  • Use your camera to see the model appear in your physical environment
  • Customize the size, color, position, and angle of the 3D model
